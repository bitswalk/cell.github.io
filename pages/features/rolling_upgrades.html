ROLLING UPGRADES

Giving you back control over your infrastructure upgrades process by using
versioned channels rolling upgrades.

At bitswalk, inc. we love rock solid infrastructures that can deliver nearly zero downtime service. However, in order to do that you need a rock solid distribution OS that will give you peace of mind for decades, not just months.

Because we believe our customers deserves the right to stay in control of their infrastructures and how they evolves, we assembled a completely opensource and free Linux distribution that bring just that. A modern, up to date and constantly evolving distribution, that let you in control of what should be updated and when.

And because we now how time is precious, we created this distribution to be fully used through a simple and wonderful user interface while being completely instrumented and monitored from scratch. We let you focus on your core business by providing you a distribution that just works, as long as you want it to work.
